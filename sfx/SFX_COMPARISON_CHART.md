## COMPARISON BY DESIGN PRINCIPLE

### Principle 1: Cultural Authenticity

**Before**:
- Generic 8-bit sounds
- Could be from Mario, Sonic, or any platformer
- No connection to Mexico, axolotls, or Xochimilco

**After**:
- Water sounds (canals, splashes, drips)
- Aztec instruments (conch shells, ceremonial drums, clay)
- Organic materials (wood, reeds, natural textures)
- Ecological sounds (birds, frogs, nature responding)

**Impact**: Game feels unique, rooted in specific place and culture

---

### Principle 2: Cohesion with Music

**Before**:
- SFX unrelated to music system
- Different instrument palette
- No shared motifs or themes

**After**:
- Uses same instruments as music (marimba, kalimba, hand drums, flutes)
- Victory sounds quote Xochi motif from music
- Key relationships match musical harmony
- Players subconsciously connect SFX to soundtrack

**Impact**: Audio feels like unified composition, professional polish

---

### Principle 3: Emotional Resonance

**Before**:
- Functional but forgettable
- No emotional arc in sounds
- Transactional (action â†’ beep)

**After**:
- Jump = playful confidence (major third, bouncy)
- Hurt = painful but hopeful (descending to resolve)
- Baby rescue = deep love (dual voices, celebration, world rejoices)
- Boss defeat = epic triumph (5 seconds of glory)

**Impact**: Players FEEL their actions, remember moments, share experiences

---

### Principle 4: Fatigue Prevention

**Before**:
- Simple repetitive sounds
- No variation (same sound every time)
- Annoying after 20+ plays

**After**:
- Pitch variation system (Â±5% random)
- Multiple recordings for footsteps
- Rich harmonic content (less fatiguing than pure tones)
- Tested for 50+ consecutive plays

**Impact**: Players don't turn SFX volume down, sounds stay fresh

## ACCESSIBILITY COMPARISON

### Visual Impairment Support

**Before**:
- Sounds don't indicate direction
- No distinction between item types (coin reused)
- Hard to navigate by audio alone

**After**:
- Stereo panning (danger left = left speaker louder)
- Distinct timbre per item (flower â‰  star â‰  powerup)
- Spatial audio for all important events
- Navigate entirely by sound possible

---

### Hearing Impairment Support

**Before**:
- Limited visual feedback
- Some actions silent

**After**:
- Every sound has visual equivalent (particles, UI, screen flash)
- Vibration patterns match sound rhythms
- Subtitle support for narrative sounds (boss roars)

---

### Sensory Sensitivity Support

**Before**:
- No volume control per category
- Low health warning could cause anxiety

**After**:
- Separate sliders: SFX / Music / Ambience
- Toggle for warning sounds (low health, danger)
- Simplified audio mode (removes high-frequency sparkles)
- No jump scares or loud surprises
